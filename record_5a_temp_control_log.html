Processes: Cook all potentially hazardous Food To at least 75°C
Reheat cooled potentially hazardous food that is to be served hot to at least 60°C

Cooked Potentially Hazardous food that is being cooled for later use must be cooled
from:
60°C to 21°C within the first 2 hours. It must then be cooled
from
21°C to below 5°C within the next 4 hours ( 6 hours in all)
